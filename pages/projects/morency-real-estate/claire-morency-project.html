Product

Claire Morency Real Estate is a North West based boutique real estate group. Driven to facilitate informed investing in a community and a future.

Project

The project consisted of the discovery, design, and production of print and digital identity.

Process

  • What are the high impact element that our clients want to know about this market?

  • How do we convey it to them in a way that respects their time?

  • What is our business objective?

  • How are we going to measure our target?

  • Design Questions?

    • Is it engaging?
    • Does it respect our clients and our brand?
    • Is it informative?
    • Does it drive toward our business objective?
    • Are we happy with our work?

Result

The successful production of a engaging, client considerate quarterly outreach strategy.
