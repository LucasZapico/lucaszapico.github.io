Product

Advanced Aircraft Company develops and produces concept drones focused on optimizing airtime, weight, and durability. The drones use solid-state fuel to enhance flight time and power output.

Project

The goal of this project was to produce a product page, anchoring AAC developing a digital identity and informing and driving potential customers into clients. Showcasing the key value of the product and the potential benefit for customers.

Process

The site followed an inform, validate, trust and convert model. To meet this need it was necessary to answer key questions;

  • Discover product values
  • Identification of audience
  • Synthesis of information into core data points and accompanying visual compliments

Result

The project resulted in the successful development, production and deplyment of the AAC Site
