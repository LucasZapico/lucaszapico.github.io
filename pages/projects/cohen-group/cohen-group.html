Product

Cohen Group is a Keller Williams Real Estate group located in Bellingham Washington. Focused on putting good people in culturally rich communities.

Project

The goal of this project was to produce a brand identity that accurately expressed professional, value focused reality service provided through the Cohen Group. This presented an opportunity to make an visual impact on customers and the community supporting a new phase of growth.

Process

  • Symbolizing, community, North West, real estate and support
  • Produce a unique personal brand while aligning with Keller William guidelines
  • Producing a brand that was recognizable as a real estate brand while being unique and personal

Competition

Questions
  • What common symbolism exists in the industry?

  • What are the pros and cons of deviating or adhering to the trend?

  • What do they feel like?

  • What is their message?

  • What is the opportunity to standout?
