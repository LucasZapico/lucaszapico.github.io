Product

Cohen Group is a Bellingham based real estate group. Devoted to building itself into a force centered around supporting and sharing with others the wonders of the Bellingham area.

Project

Give body to the Cohen Groups core values through a brand identity.

Process

Ask questions that lead to stories

  • What is the brand purpose?

  • How are we driven to enrich the community?

  • How do we express this through our content?

  • What are the expectation of our audience?

     

Result

The project resulted in a distinctly North West mark, complemented by Raleway's typography in a medium weight adding a refined tone.
