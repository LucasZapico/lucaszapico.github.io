Product

Cohen Group is a Bellingham based real estate group. Devoted to builing itself into a force centered around supporting and sharing with others the wonders of the Bellingham area.

Project

The goal of this project was to give body to the Cohen Groups core values through a brand identity. Discoverying and conveying the key values of the business.

Process

  • What are the brands core values?

  • What is the brands purpose and mission?

  • How do we express this through our content?

  • What if any are the exprectations of our audience?

     

Result

The project resulted in a distinctly North West mark, complimeted by Raleway typography in a medium weight adding a refined tone.
