Product

The DadApp was a task and schedule management tool for new fathers to keep track of their busy schedules.

Project

The goal was to produce a semi-programmable application that would allow the user to track, check and repeat baby-related tasks.

Process

The application was developed radially with a central hub allowing specific features to be accessed.

  • Discovery of core product features
  • Production of low-fidelity, wireframes and high-fidelity prototypes
  • Synthesis of design into native IOS application

Result

Successful development, production, and deployment of the DadApp MVP to the Apple store.
