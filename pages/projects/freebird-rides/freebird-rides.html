Product

Freebird Rides is a digital customer acquisition market place that allows businesses to pay for Uber rides for customers to there locations. Leveraging a suite of technologies to provide direct return on investment metrics to the business.

Project

responsibilities included production of physical and digital brand assets including mobile, web user interfaces, and marketing marterials. The quality assurance testing and reporting of product functionality.

Process

Since many of the challenges faced were highly nuanced. Each was tackled meticulously, requiring insight and deliberation from all members of the team. Due to the, stage of growth of the product, the unique suite of technologies used and user cultures in play. The process generally applied against any problem was a check against a series of questions.

  • What is the priority with respect to the rest of the product?
  • Is there a solution in place?
  • What data is available against the problem?
  • What data or information is needed?
  • What is the cost of acquiring this data or information?

Result

The experience resulted in the production of five iterations of the product from minimul viable product too a stable version five.
