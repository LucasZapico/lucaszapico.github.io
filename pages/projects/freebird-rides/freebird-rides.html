Product

Freebird Rides is a digital customer acquisition marketplace that allows businesses to pay for Uber rides for customers to there locations. Leveraging a suite of technologies to provide a direct return on investment metrics to the business.

Project

Produce physical and digital brand assets including mobile, web user interfaces, and marketing materials. Test quality assurance and report on product functionality.

Check out an early Freebird Rides prototype here

Process

Since many of the challenges faced were highly nuanced. Each was tackled meticulously, requiring insight and deliberation from all members of the team. Due to the, stage of growth of the product, the unique suite of technologies used and user cultures in play. The process generally applied against any problem was a check against a series of questions.

  • What is the priority with respect to the rest of the product?

  • Is there a solution in place?

  • What data is available against the problem?

  • What data or information is needed?

  • What is the cost of acquiring this data or information?

Result

The experience resulted in the production of multiple product iterations and deployments from minimal viable product to stable scaleable full production versions.

Deliverables

  • Print Design

  • User flow Diagraming

  • Quality Assurance Testing
