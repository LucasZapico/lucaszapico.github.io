Product

Lifelines was a post drug treatment recovery suppliment concept. The product was meant to use digital channels and a feedback system to keep recoveries accountable and supporters in touch.

Project

The project consisted of the discovery, planning, design and frontend development.

Process

  • Disocovery and refinement of key product features
  • Prioritizing of feature hierarchy
  • Architecture of application flow and user interactions
  • Production of low, mid and high-fidelity prototypes of web and mobile application views and interactions
  • Development of react frontend

Result

The project resulted in the successful design and development of the lifelines frontend.
