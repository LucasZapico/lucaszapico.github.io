Product

Lifelines was a post-drug treatment recovery supplement concept. The product was meant to use digital channels and a feedback system to keep recoveries accountable and supporters in touch.

Project

The project consisted of the discovery, design, architecture and frontend development.

Process

What we asked?

  • What value are we providing first and foremost?

  • How do we deliver that value in a succinct way?

  • How does our architecture follow this?

  • How do we intend to validate or disprove our assumptions?

Result

The project resulted in the successful design and development of the lifelines frontend.

Deliverables

  • Application Architecture

  • Design Direction

  • User Interface and Experience Design

  • Frontend Web Development
