Product

Cohen Group is a Keller Williams Real Estate group located in Bellingham Washington. Focused on putting good people in healthy communities their by facilitating the meeting of individual goals.

Project

The goal of this project is to produce a brand identity that will accurately express key brand values of community, support, growth and the North West. This presented a unique opportunity to be involved in laying the a piece of the business foundation for a new phase of growth and ease the development of brand content digital and static.

Interesting Challenges...
  • Symbolizing, community, North West, real estate and support
  • Produce a unique personal brand while aligning with Keller William guidelines
  • Producing a brand that was recognizable as a real estate brand while being unique and personal
