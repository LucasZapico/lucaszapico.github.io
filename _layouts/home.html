The workshop is where I play around and develop the various skill required to produce digital products. This is a little snapshot into my development process. In that, I produce and iterate on modules as a drafting and reference repository to build out projects. My workshop is broken into two sections.

Section one contains projects. As defined as an object composed of modules that produces value and a clearly defined completions point.

Section two is a showcase of modules and playgrounds that I have built through Codepen during the development of a project.
